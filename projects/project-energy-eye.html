Project Summary

Led the development of "Energy Eye," a commercial product designed to reduce energy monitoring installation costs and provide actionable financial insights for business owners through innovative IoT architecture and intuitive UI/UX design.

Removing Barriers to Adoption

I identified that high installation costs were preventing commercial clients from adopting energy monitoring. To solve this, I directed the engineering team to utilize Non-Intrusive Optical Interfaces and Sigfox 0G Networks. This architectural decision eliminated the need for wiring or WiFi, reducing installation time by 90%.

Vendor Coordination & Quality Control

Managed the end-to-end hardware development lifecycle across external vendors. I defined strict technical requirements for a 'Zero-Infrastructure' device, pushing for a 2-year battery life and standalone connectivity to ensure the product was scalable for mass market deployment.

Designing for Business Value

Led the UI/UX design phase for the mobile application. I focused on translating raw engineering data (kWh) into financial insights (Cost per Day) that business owners could actually use. I designed the 'Peak vs. Off-Peak' visualization to help users identify specific times to cut costs.
