Integrated Smart Factory Ecosystem (IIoT)

Role: System Integration Lead | Timeline: 2023 - Present

Hardware & Automation: Tridium JACE 8000 PLC Industrial Panel Design Vibration & Proximity Sensors
Software & Protocols: Niagara Framework (Supervisor) SQL Database Integration Modbus RTU & BACnet/IP SMS Gateway Logic
Management & Business: Vendor Management Quotation & Pricing Strategy Stakeholder Communication Technical & User Documentation

Project Abstract

Architected a centralized IIoT ecosystem to digitize the entire factory. Unified 4 distinct operational silos (Quality, Maintenance, Production, Facility) into a single Niagara Supervisor platform, feeding a central SQL Database for analytics.

Module Breakdown

Module A (Quality)

9 Firing Machines | Modbus Integration

Goal: Maximize Yield

Module B (Maintenance)

Vibration Monitoring | Predictive Analysis

Goal: Zero Unplanned Downtime

Module C (Production)

38 Machines | Auto-OEE Tracking

Goal: 100% Automated Data

Module D (Facility)

Intelligent HVAC | Occupancy Control

Goal: 15% Energy Savings

Industrial Panel Design & Wiring

Custom PLC Control Panel with Mitsubishi FX5U and neat wire labeling.

Tridium JACE 8000 Gateway integrating RS485 Modbus and IP networks.

Real-Time Visualization & Analytics

Automated OEE Dashboard: Real-time tracking of Availability, Performance, and Quality for 14 production lines.

Facility Digital Twin: Floor plan mapping of AC units with live status and occupancy control.

High-Density Zone Monitoring: Live temperature tracking across all firing zones with deviation alarms.
