About Me

I’m a tinkerer at heart. My career really kicked off with the excitement of a drone startup, and that got me hooked on the whole tech stack—from wiring up hardware to writing the code that brings it to life. I realized pretty quickly that the coolest innovations happen when you bridge the gap between the physical world and the digital one.

Basically, I love making machines 'talk and think.' There’s nothing better than taking a messy, manual process and turning it into a smooth, automated system. I live for that 'aha!' moment when a client sees their data on a screen for the first time and realizes everything just got easier.

Some of the stuff I’m proudest of:

  • Factory IoT: Built a system that stopped machines from breaking down constantly (cut downtime by 80%).
  • Hardware Design: Engineered a water quality sensor that worked perfectly but cost 75% less than the big brands.
  • Entrepreneurship: Built a self-service laundry business from scratch and automated the operations so it basically runs itself.

I’m always looking for the next hard problem to solve. If you’re into building smarter systems or just want to geek out about automation, let’s connect.
